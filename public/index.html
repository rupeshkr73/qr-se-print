QR Se Print kya hai?

QR Se Print cyber cafe aur print shop ke liye ek automatic print software hai. Aap apni shop ka QR code lagate ho — customer apne phone se scan karke file upload karta hai, payment karta hai, aur print aapke printer se automatic nikal jata hai. Na WhatsApp pe file mangni padti hai, na pendrive, na email.

Customer file kaise bhejta hai?

Shop par laga QR code scan karo → file select karo (PDF, photo — ek saath kai files) → edit karo (crop, rotate, brightness) → payment karo (online ya counter cash) → print automatic nikal jata hai. Poora kaam customer ke phone se, 1 minute me.

WhatsApp pe file lene se ye better kyun hai?

WhatsApp me aapka personal number public ho jata hai, chat me files kho jaati hain, aur hisaab manually rakhna padta hai. QR Se Print me: number private rehta hai, har print ka payment record automatic banta hai, aur customer ki file print ke 90 minute baad khud delete ho jaati hai — privacy bhi, hisaab bhi.

Kaunse printer ke saath chalta hai?

Har Windows printer ke saath — HP, Canon, Epson, Brother, sab. B&W aur Color ke liye alag printer set kar sakte ho. Advance me 4×6 photo printer, A3 bada printer aur duplex (dono side) printing bhi support hai.

Payment kaise milta hai? Koi commission?

Do tarike: counter par cash, ya online payment (Razorpay/PhonePe) jo seedha AAPKE account me jata hai. Hum beech me nahi aate — koi commission nahi, unlimited prints.

Kitna kharcha aata hai?

Free demo se shuru karo (bina paisa diye try karo). Phir ₹399/month ka monthly plan ya ₹999 one-time lifetime plan — ek baar do, hamesha chalao. Koi hidden charge nahi, koi per-print commission nahi.

Internet chala jaye to print ka kya hoga?

Customer ke jobs queue me safe rehte hain. Internet wapas aate hi software khud jobs utha ke print nikal deta hai — kuch khota nahi.

What QR Se Print does

QR Se Print connects a customer’s mobile phone directly to a shop’s printer. The shop displays its own QR code; the customer scans it, uploads files, pays, and the job prints automatically through the Windows print-relay agent installed at the shop.

The Platform includes multi-file printing (several PDFs and photos in one order), on-phone editing such as crop, rotate and brightness, automatic 4×6 passport photo sheets with cutting lines, a built-in resume maker, A3/A2/A1 and duplex support, and an offline queue that releases jobs once the internet returns. Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026 qrseprint.in — Files delete turant · Shop owner ke apne logs
QR Se Print is built around a simple promise: a customer’s document should exist only long enough to print, and nothing longer. This policy explains exactly what qrseprint.in collects, how long a print file survives, and what a shop owner can see. We do not sell data to anyone, and we do not keep a library of your customers’ documents.
Saral bhasha meSabse zaroori baat: customer ki file print hote hi turant delete ho jati hai — server par kuch nahi bachta. Shop owner apne admin panel ke logs me dekh sakta hai ki job print hua aur file hat gayi. Card ya UPI PIN hum kabhi store nahi karte, wo Razorpay ke paas jata hai.

Print files are deleted immediately after printing

This is the core of how QR Se Print works. When a customer scans a shop’s QR code on qrseprint.in and uploads a document, that file is held only until the print-relay agent has sent it to the shop’s printer. As soon as the job is printed, the file is deleted right away — it is not archived, not backed up, and not kept for later viewing by us or by the shop.

A job that is never paid for, or never printed, is cleared automatically as well, so nothing lingers on qrseprint.in indefinitely. Because deletion is immediate, a printed file cannot be recovered or reprinted from the server — if a customer needs another copy, they must upload it again. Customers should always keep their own copy of any document they send.

What the shop owner can see in their logs

Every shop has its own admin panel on qrseprint.in with a print log. That log records what happened, not the document itself: job ID and time, number of pages and copies, colour or black-and-white, the amount paid and payment status, and whether the file was printed and cleared.

The shop owner can therefore verify their earnings and confirm that each customer file was removed after printing — while the document content itself is already gone. Only that shop can see its own log; one shop can never see another shop’s jobs or customers.

Information we collect

Beyond the print file itself, qrseprint.in collects only what the service needs to work:

  • Shop account — shop name, owner name, mobile number, email, shop area, Shop ID, and password stored in hashed form;
  • Print job record — page count, copies, colour setting, paper size, amount, payment status and time;
  • Customer mobile number, where entered, so the right job can be identified at the counter;
  • Payment reference — transaction ID, amount and success or failure, received from the payment gateway;
  • Printer list from the shop computer — the names of installed printers, so you can choose which printer handles which job;
  • Basic technical logs — device type, browser, agent version, error messages and timestamps.

We do not read, index, or analyse the contents of customer documents, and we never use them for advertising, profiling, or training any model.

Payment details are never stored with us

Online payments on qrseprint.in are handled by Razorpay or the shop’s own UPI. Card numbers, CVV, UPI PIN and net-banking passwords are entered on the payment provider’s own secure screen. QR Se Print never sees or stores them. We receive only the transaction ID, amount, and whether it succeeded — which is what releases the print job.

Who else sees anything

We do not sell or rent personal information. Limited data is shared only where the service requires it:

  • The shop you sent the job to — which receives the print job and the counter details needed to hand it over;
  • Razorpay — to take and verify payment;
  • Our hosting and database providers — to run qrseprint.in;
  • Authorities — only where a valid legal requirement applies.

Security

We use reasonable technical measures to protect qrseprint.in, including encrypted connections, hashed passwords, admin access controls, and shop-level separation so no shop can reach another shop’s data. Immediate deletion of print files is itself a security measure — data that no longer exists cannot leak.

No system is perfectly secure, and we cannot promise absolute safety. Shop owners must protect their own Shop ID, password, and the computer running the print-relay agent.

Cookies and local storage

qrseprint.in uses cookies and browser local storage to keep a shop owner signed in to the admin panel, remember the language chosen on the homepage, and record basic errors so faults can be fixed. These are functional. You can clear or block them in your browser, though the admin panel may then not stay logged in.

Your rights

You may, subject to Indian law:

  • Ask what personal information we hold about your shop account;
  • Ask us to correct anything wrong;
  • Ask us to delete your shop account and its details, where we are not required to keep them;
  • Ask us to stop sending non-essential messages.

Send any such request through the Business Inquiry form on qrseprint.in or the contact details in the footer.

Children

QR Se Print is meant for shop owners and their walk-in customers. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If you believe a child’s information has reached us, tell us and we will remove it.

Changes to this policy

If the way qrseprint.in handles data changes, this page is updated and the date at the top changes with it. Anything that materially affects shops will also be shown in the admin panel.
